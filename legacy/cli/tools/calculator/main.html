Usage


Overview

There are many things you can do with this calculator.
There are many functions you can use, and you can put just about any JavaScript-compatible expression that resolves to a number.


Basic Arithmetic

+ is addition, - is subtraction, * is multiplication, / is division
Examples: 1+2 results in 3, 5-2 results in 3, 2*3 results in 6, and 6/6 results in 1


Result Usage

If you hover over the Equals button, you will notice that there are more options than just '='.
Each option will take the expression from the input and output their function.
Example: 5 + 5 -> Is Prime will result in 'false', because 5 + 5 (10) is not prime
